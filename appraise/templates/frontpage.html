METEVAL

Dear annotator, the objective of METEVAL is to confirm the ability of our in-house designed procedure to discriminate the most informative translation within a list of translation candidates. The data to be given to you consists of an either English, German or Czech sentence associated with a list of translation candidates in on the of the others two languages. Your contribution in this experiment is thus to rank the list of translation candidates from the best (rank1) to the worse (rank5) according to the given source sentence.
